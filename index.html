Welcome to the Danish Atlantic Council

We are dedicated towards educating people about the invaluable strategic work carried out within the Denmark-NATO partnership, fostering understanding and cooperation for global as well as national security.

Our Core Goals and Values

  • Foster understanding of the Denmark-NATO strategic partnership.
  • Promote European security through collaborative security coordination.
  • Educate on the value of cooperative defense strategies.
  • Encourage innovation in security policy and international cooperation.

Engage With Us

  • Attend our seminars and lectures across the country.
  • Participate in expert panels with leading security experts.
  • Collaborate in shaping the future of European collective defense.
